About Me


I'm Mariah, a passionate aspiring web developer, artist, and self-proclaimed coffee addict ☕

I'm currently honing my skills in a Software Development program with Correlation One, where I'm immersing myself in the exciting world of web development. But there's so much more to me than lines of code and pixels on a screen.


Art and Creativity


Art has always been an essential part of my life. I find immense joy in bringing my imagination to life through drawing and creating various forms of art.

From intricate pencil sketches to vibrant paintings, I love exploring different mediums and styles to express my creativity. Art allows me to delve into a world of boundless possibilities, where I can let my imagination soar.


Wake Forest, NC


When I'm not immersed in the realms of art and technology, you'll find me in Wake Forest, North Carolina, where I call home.

This charming town nestled in the heart of the state provides me with a serene and peaceful environment to recharge and draw inspiration from nature's beauty. It's a place where I can escape the bustling city life and find solace in the tranquility of the countryside.


Proudly Serving in the Navy


In addition to my artistic pursuits and web development training, I proudly serve in the Navy.

The Navy has instilled in me a sense of discipline, determination, and a strong work ethic. The experiences and values I've gained through my service have shaped me into the person I am today, and I'm grateful for the opportunities it has provided me.


Working at Amazon


On a professional front, I'm fortunate to work at Amazon, where I contribute to the dynamic world of e-commerce. Collaborating with a team of talented individuals, I'm continuously learning and growing in this fast-paced and innovative environment.

It's a thrilling experience to be part of a company that revolutionizes the way people shop and connects individuals from all walks of life.


Connect with Me!

Additionally, I'll be sharing insights, tips, and resources on web development, as I strive to empower and inspire aspiring developers like myself.
